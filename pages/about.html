Why?

I like physics, teaching people, and organizing knowledge. These notes represent my journey through graduate-level physics courses, organized for easy reference and review.

The Goal:

People come here to gain a better understanding of physics through my organized course notes. The goal is to create a comprehensive, searchable resource that makes complex physics concepts more accessible through clear explanations and mathematical derivations.

Course Coverage:

Currently includes notes from these WPI physics courses:

  • PH 511 - Classical Mechanics (Prof. Zozulya)
  • PH 514 - Quantum Mechanics I (Prof. Aravind)
  • PH 541 - Mathematical Methods (Prof. Zozulya)
